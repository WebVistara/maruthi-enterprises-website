Safety & Quality

Our commitment to zero-harm, excellence, and compliance drives every project.

OUR STANDARDS

Safety First, Quality Always

We integrate rigorous safety protocols and quality checks into every phase of our work.

Safety Policy

We maintain a strict safety-first culture. Every employee undergoes regular training, and all sites follow mandatory PPE, toolbox talks, and risk assessments. We aim for zero lost-time incidents.

ISO 45001 aligned

Quality Assurance

Our QA process includes material inspection, vendor audits, and in-process checks. We adhere to ISO 9001 principles to ensure every installation meets or exceeds specifications.

ISO 9001 practices

Electrical Safety Compliance

We strictly follow Indian Electricity Rules, CEIG regulations, and client safety protocols. All installations are tested for earthing, insulation, and protection before energization.

CEIG / IE Rules

Environmental Responsibility

We are committed to environmentally responsible operations. Our teams follow strict guidelines for waste management, oil handling, and pollution prevention at all project sites.

ISO 14001 Principles

Testing & Inspection

Comprehensive testing: thermography, megger, relay calibration, earth resistance, and pressure gauge calibration. We document all results for client records.

NABL traceable equipment

Industrial Safety Procedures

Permit-to-work systems, lockout-tagout, confined space entry procedures, and emergency response plans are implemented on all sites. Regular drills and safety audits ensure readiness.
