About Maruthi Enterprises

Government licensed electrical contractor with 15+ years of industrial excellence – delivering HT/LT solutions up to 33KV across India.

WHO WE ARE

Introduction

Maruthi Enterprises has been operating in the electrical field for over 15 years and is a Bengaluru-based government licensed electrical contractor. We provide comprehensive HT and LT electrical services including site surveys, electrical system design, preparation of BOQs, and tenders for industrial clients.

Our team combines technical expertise with safety-first approach, ensuring every project meets stringent quality standards. We serve pharmaceutical, food, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors with pan-India delivery.
